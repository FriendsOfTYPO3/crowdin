..  _what-it-does:

What does it do?
================

The extension makes a connection between your TYPO3 instance and your account at Crowdin and

*   Enable you to make inplace translate TYPO3 core and available TYPO3 extensions
*   Enable you to see and translate labels directly in both backend and frontend
*   Give you access to the same tools as when translating in Crowdin (Translation Memory, Glossary, etc)
